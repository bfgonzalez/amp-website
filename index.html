Who are we?

The Ateneo Musicians' Pool (AMP) is a non-profit organization where individuals realize their holistic formation through music.

Mission

We commit ourselves to excel in music production, development and appreciation by creating projects and structures that support members in enhancing their talents and fulfilling their pursuits.

DEPARTMENTS

Artist Management (AM)

Workshops, jam sessions, and live performances. This department is devoted to bringing out the best in AMP's musicians in skill, work ethic, attitude, and professionalism.

Design (DD)

The visual artists. They give the organization a stronger visual identity through its posters, logos, album covers, and other paraphernalia.

Documentations & Publications (DP)

The photographers, writers and videographers. They commemorate the experience that AMP creates -- they make history.

Sales & Sponsorships (S&S)

Raises revenue and the exposure necessary to launch the organization's projects. In charge of fundraisers, producing merchandise, and expanding corporate relations.

Sound Engineering (SE)

In charge of the sound production during projects and events, sound equipment of the organization, as well as the rentals and services being offered.

Strategic Planning of Promotions (SPP)

Deals with the promotion of AMP's artists and events by optimizing the available resources for promotion through innovative and creative ideas.
