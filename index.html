What is AMP all about?

The Ateneo Musicians' Pool (AMP) is a non-profit organization where individuals realize their holistic formation through music.

VISION

We aim to foster a collaborative environment amongst personal member efforts outside official projects and structures to encourage their independent development and to help fulfill their pursuits and interests.

MISSION

We dedicate ourselves to excel in music production, development and appreciation by creating projects and structures that support members in enhancing their talents and fulfilling their pursuits.

We advocate our membersâ€™ holistic formation and contribute to the transformation of the Philippine society by creating a view of human existence where music and the arts are essential.

Departments

Artist Management (AM)

Devoted to the wholesome development of AMP's musicians in order to bring out the best in its members in skill, work ethic, attitude, and professionalism. These areas are worked on during workshops, jam sessions, live performances, and much more.

Design (DD)

The visual artists. They give the organization a stronger visual identity through its posters, logos, album covers, and other paraphernalia.

Documentations and Publications (DP)

The photographers, writers and videographers. They commemorate the experience that AMP creates. In short, they make history.

Sales and Sponsorships (S&S)

The business-minded side of the organization that aims to raise revenues and exposure necessary to launch the organization's projects. In charge of fundraisers, producing merchandise, and expanding corporate relations to support the organization's goal in spreading music.

Sound Engineering (SE)

The sound producers. In charge of the sound production during projects and events, sound equipment of the organization, as well as the rentals and services being offered.

Strategic Planning of Promotions (SPP)

Deals with the promotion of AMP's artists and events by optimizing the available resources for promotion through innovative and creative ideas.
